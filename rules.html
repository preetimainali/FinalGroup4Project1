Rules & Safety

Your safety and trust are our top priorities. Learn about our community guidelines and safety measures.

Community Guidelines

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with kindness and respect
  • Use appropriate language and avoid offensive content
  • Respect different backgrounds, cultures, and perspectives
  • Communicate clearly and professionally

Be Honest

  • Provide accurate descriptions of tasks and services
  • Set realistic expectations for completion times
  • Be transparent about your skills and experience
  • Honor your commitments and agreements

Be Safe

  • Meet in public places for in-person tasks
  • Never share personal financial information
  • Report suspicious or inappropriate behavior
  • Use our platform's messaging system for communication

Safety Measures

User Verification

We verify user identities through email confirmation and optional phone verification to ensure authentic accounts.

Secure Messaging

All communications happen through our secure platform messaging system to protect your privacy.

Rating System

Our rating and review system helps you make informed decisions about who to work with.

Report System

Easy reporting system for inappropriate behavior, with quick response from our moderation team.

Prohibited Activities

Important Notice

The following activities are strictly prohibited and may result in account suspension or termination:

  • Illegal Activities: Any task involving illegal activities or substances
  • Dangerous Work: Tasks that could cause physical harm or require professional licenses
  • Academic Dishonesty: Completing academic assignments, exams, or coursework for others
  • Spam or Scams: Fraudulent activities, spam, or misleading information
  • Inappropriate Content: Adult content, harassment, or hate speech
  • Platform Abuse: Creating multiple accounts or manipulating the rating system

Need Help?

Contact Our Safety Team

If you encounter any safety concerns or need to report inappropriate behavior, our team is here to help.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: December 2024

What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: These are necessary for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled
  • Performance Cookies: These help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functionality Cookies: These enable enhanced functionality and personalization
  • Analytics Cookies: These help us analyze website traffic and usage patterns

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Session Cookies Maintain your login session Until browser closes
Preference Cookies Remember your settings and preferences 30 days
Analytics Cookies Track website usage and performance 2 years

Managing Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our site are set by third-party services that we use, such as Google Analytics. These cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of these third parties.

Community Guidelines

These guidelines help create a positive, safe, and productive environment for all users of GetItDone.

Be Respectful

  • Treat all users with kindness and respect
  • Use appropriate language and tone in all communications
  • Be patient and understanding with other students
  • Respect different backgrounds, opinions, and experiences

Be Honest and Transparent

  • Provide accurate descriptions of tasks and your abilities
  • Communicate clearly about expectations and timelines
  • Be upfront about any limitations or challenges
  • Honor your commitments and agreements

Be Safe

  • Meet in public places for in-person tasks
  • Never share personal financial information
  • Report any suspicious or inappropriate behavior
  • Trust your instincts and prioritize your safety

Be Professional

  • Respond to messages in a timely manner
  • Complete tasks to the best of your ability
  • Provide fair and honest feedback
  • Resolve conflicts respectfully and constructively

Prohibited Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:
  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
  • Discrimination based on race, gender, religion, or other protected characteristics
  • Spam, scams, or fraudulent activities
  • Sharing inappropriate or offensive content
  • Academic dishonesty or cheating
  • Violation of university policies or local laws

Reporting Violations

If you witness or experience behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it immediately using our reporting system or by contacting our safety team at safety@getitdone.com.

Consequences

Violations of these guidelines may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or permanent account suspension, depending on the severity and frequency of the violation.
