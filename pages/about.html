About GetItDone

Connecting students with small jobs to make life easier and earn extra income.

Our Mission

We believe that small tasks shouldn't be a big burden. GetItDone connects students who need help with everyday tasks to other students who are willing to help for fair compensation. Together, we're building a supportive community where everyone can thrive.

Our Story

GetItDone was born from a simple observation: college students often struggle with balancing academics, work, and personal responsibilities. Small tasks like grocery shopping, pet sitting, or moving assistance can become overwhelming when you're already juggling coursework and part-time jobs.

Our founders, a group of Management Information Systems (MIS) students at the University of Alabama, experienced this challenge firsthand. They realized that many students had skills and time to help with these tasks, while others needed assistance but couldn't afford professional services.

Today, GetItDone serves as a bridge between students who need help and those who can provide it, creating a supportive ecosystem where everyone benefits.

Our Values

Community First

We prioritize building a supportive, inclusive community where students help each other succeed.

Safety & Trust

Your safety and security are our top priorities. We verify users and provide secure communication channels.

Fair Compensation

We believe in fair pay for fair work, ensuring both task posters and helpers are compensated appropriately.
